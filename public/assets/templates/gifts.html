Gifts

The very best gift we can receive is your presence at our wedding, as well as your love and support as we embark on this adventure together.

We recognize that especially for those of you traveling long distances, simply attending our wedding will already entail spending money on transportation and accommodations. Homemade, handmade, and second-hand gifts are welcome and appreciated.

Also, many of you are contributing significant time and energy to this event, and for that we are more grateful than we'll ever be able to convey.

Charitable Gifts

If you're just coming for the free lobster and open bar, in lieu of a wedding gift, consider donating a few bucks towards these causes that we deeply and honestly believe are changing the world for the better.

  • Word Made Flesh Thailand works against exploitation, believing that all lives are intrinsically valuable because all are created by God in the image of God.

  • Ubuntu Education Fund is guided by a simple, all-encompassing, yet radical mission: to help raise Port Elizabeth, South Africa’s orphaned and vulnerable children by giving them what all children deserve—everything. See more at ubuntufund.org.

  • TinySuperheroes seeks to empower Extraordinary kids — one cape at a time. They send capes to Extraordinary TinySuperheroes who exemplify strength and determination as they overcome illness or disability.
