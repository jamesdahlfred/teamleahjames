While you're in the area…

You should take advantage of the nearby gorgeous beaches, oceanside walking trails, whale watching boat tours, and quaint New England towns in which to eat, shop, and sightsee.

Some of our favorite spots include:

  • Singing Beach in Manchester and Good Harbor Beach and Magnolia Beach in Gloucester
  • Bear Skin Neck in Rockport (painfully cute oceanside town)
  • Russell Orchard in Ipswich offers great apple picking and cider doughnuts
  • The Peabody Essex Museum and the Salem Witch Museum for a bit of culture and history
  • The Coolidge Estate in Manchester for a scenic and relaxing coastal walk
